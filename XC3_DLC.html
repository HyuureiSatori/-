Xenoblade Chronicles: Future Redeemed

Xenoblade Chronicles 3: Future Redeemed (Japanese: ゼノブレイド３　新たなる未来, Zenobureido 3 Aratanaru mirai) is an expansion to Xenoblade Chronicles 3 published by Nintendo and developed by Monolith Soft for the Nintendo Switch. It was first teased during the February 8, 2023 Nintendo Direct, and later officially announced on April 18, 2023. It was released as part of Wave 4 of the Xenoblade Chronicles 3 Expansion Pass on April 25, 2023 in North America and on April 26, 2023 everywhere else.
